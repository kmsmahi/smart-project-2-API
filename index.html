About Our Campaign

Small Seeds, Global Impact

Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. Since our start, we’ve planted over 500,000 trees worldwide.

By joining our campaign, you help restore forests, create habitats for wildlife, and combat global warming. Every tree planted is a step toward a cooler, greener planet.

  • ✓ Restoration of natural habitats
  • ✓ Improvement of air quality
  • ✓ Support for local communities
